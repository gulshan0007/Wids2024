WHAT IS iSURP?

iSURP(in-Semester Undergraduate Research Programme) as the name suggests is a centralized process for project allocation for research enthusiastic undergraduate students during the winters and spring semester. This helps them to undertake projects in their own departments and also helps in launching inter-departmental projects in their areas of interest. It encourages students to take up hands-on research experiences under IIT Bombay professors and their teams during the semester. This program is majorly for UG Students to explore the field of research in their department of interest. For more information please refer to the Rule Booklet.

iSURP: SELECTION PROCEDURE

Stage 1: SOP Shortlisting by iSURP Team as well as Professors. Please fill in your general motivation to apply and your previous work experience in the application form. These questions will help the Professors and Team iSURP gauge whether a student is really driven and will see the project through. Project-specific SOPs will help Professors gauge students’ interest. Timeline: 22nd November, 2022 to 25th November, 2022
Stage 2 : Professors might request for interviews(virtual) or tests for shortlisted candidates. Timeline: 26th November, 2022 to 30th November, 2022
All selections will be declared by the first week of December.
All dates mentioned are tentative and may differ from project to project.

FAQ

How do I apply for projects?

  1. Go to the iSURP Portal and click on the APPLY NOW tab on the top right corner

  2. You will be redirected to iSURP registration google form , You can only access the registration form through the IITB G-SUITE account

  3. In this form you will need to enter your personal details ,prefered project id and SOP's Link.

How many projects can I apply for? Will I be able to update my choices after choosing the projects once?

You can apply to a maximum of 3 projects from any area. Specify the preference order from 1 to 3, with 1st as the highest and 3rd as the lowest preference. Update your choices by the following steps:

  1. Go to the iSURP Portal and click on the APPLY NOW tab in the top right corner.

  2. If you have filled the form once, you will get the option to edit your response

  3. You can now update all your choices and submit new SOPs.

What should I write in an SOP?

Statement of Purpose should reflect the motivation with which you are filling the application for the project you wish to do. It is used by the professor to judge if you are enthusiastic enough to learn and work for the entire duration of the project

What is the duration of iSURP?

The duration of iSURP is a mutual decision between the professor and you. You can put forth your convenient dates during the interview and the professor can comment on his/her availability. Ideally, it is one and a half to three months, starting during winter break.. It can extend into the semester in case the student is enthusiastic and wants to work. The projects will tentatively begin in the first week of December.

In case of any query, what is supposed to be done?

Please put your questions in the query section accessible by the “More Questions?” button in the FAQs Section. We will get back to you as soon as possible. In case of instant doubts please contact ISAA EnPoWER or any of the iSURP team members whose details you can find on the contact tab on the top right corner.

What would be the penalty if I leave the project in between?

Any student who fails to perform satisfactorily in the project and/or leaves the project without the consent of the Professor-in-charge will be blacklisted in all further initiatives by UGAC. They will not receive any certification for the program.
